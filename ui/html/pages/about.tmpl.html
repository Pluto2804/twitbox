About Twitbox

Welcome to Twitbox, a modern micro-social platform built for creators, thinkers, and storytellers. We are focused on providing a simple and meaningful space that values authenticity and expression. Our platform is engineered for lightning-fast performance using Go and is secure by design, featuring robust session authentication, CSRF protection, and a privacy-first architecture. We're glad to have you as part of the journey.
